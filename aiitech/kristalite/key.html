 Ram Vairavan: Chairman & CEO


 With graduate degrees in Biomedical Engineering and an MBA in Pharmaceutical marketing, Mr. Vairavan has been active in the healthcare industry for the past 30 years. He worked with the multinational company Hoechst AG of Germany in various executive positions in Research, Product Development and Manufacturing in the USA, Germany & Singapore. In 1998 he founded a company in California engaged in the manufacturing and distribution of clinical diagnostic reagents and instrumentation for performing blood tests to diagnose complex diseases such as Myocardial Infarction and Stroke.

 

Alagu Vairavan : President

With a bachelor's degree in business administration from Newbury College in Boston Massachusetts, Alagu Vairavan has worked in a wide spectrum of market segments, from manufacturing of Mary Kay cosmetics in London, England to network marketing as associate supervisor in Shaklee corporation with special focus on Nutritional products. With experience in managing retail companies and dealing with manufacturers Alagu fully understands the needs of companies and strives to maintain a cordial relationship with customers.
