Ordering
Please e-mail or fax your purchase order to Kristalite Imports. The information sent   should include the product catalog number, product descriptions and prices. Kristalite Imports  will send you an e-mail or fax confirming your purchase order along with an estimated ship date.

Shipping Information
All orders are shipped FOB Kristalite Imports, Escondido, California, U.S.A. Shipping and handling costs are prepaid and added to the invoice unless otherwise requested by the customer. Kristalite reserves the right to select the packaging and shipping method for your order if you cannot provide adequate shipping, this is to ensure the stability of the product and also efficient tracing. Kristalite Imports will ship your orders using your preferred carrier. If the carrier is not specified, Kristalite Imports will use the most cost effective and timely service available. Your shipping address will be needed along with a contact name and phone number. UPS, FedEx, DHL and most other freight companies will not deliver to P.O. Box addresses; a street address is needed.

Return Policy
If the product purchased does not perform to specifications, please contact Kristalite   Imports immediately so we can address and correct the problem as necessary. For purposes of quality control we will need the port number, the product name and the quantity received.   If an error by Kristalite Imports results in shipment of an incorrect order, Kristalite will, at its option, either ship a replacement order at no charge or credit the customer's account for the original product shipped in error. If an error by the customer results in the shipment of an incorrect order and is reported to Kristalite Imports within 10 days, the customer may obtain returned goods authorization and return the product(s) for partial credit. You will then receive a Return Authorization Number from Kristalite Imports prior to returning the products. We do   not issue full credits for returned goods since our products are perishable and we do not resell them as we are not assured of the quality of returned goods. Refunds and credits are given at the discretion of Kristalite Imports. Kristalite Imports cannot be responsible for products that are expired, performed improperly, or stored incorrectly.
