Kristalite Imports, founded in 1995 in Boston,  Massachusetts is a distributor focused in  the  area of healthcare with special emphasis on  Raw herbs, herbal extracts and intermediates 
 and contract  formulations.

The company has since relocated to San Diego California in 1998. In December 2001,  Kristalite Imports has been named as the exclusive distributor in the US for Siris Impex, a leading manufacturer of herbal products in South India.
