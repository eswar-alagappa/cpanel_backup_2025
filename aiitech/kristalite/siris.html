SIRIS IMPEX located in Vijayawada, India, is committed to excellence in health care  through high quality products conforming to GMP standards. Utilizing the state of art infrastructure, experience and expertise of over 5 decades, coupled with the zeal to offer  the best, has given SIRIS IMPEX the edge over others, and has become a name to reckon  with in the  international market scene. This resulted in exports herbal extracts, raw herbs  (seeds, roots, leaves, fruits, whole plants etc.) and herbal formulations on contract  manufacturing basis  to countries like Australia, Belgium, Canada, Denmark, France,  Germany, Italy, Japan, South  Africa, Sweden, Switzerland, Thailand, U.K., USA, etc.

Research forms an integral part of the company. Adherence to stringent quality control  norms further intensifies the role of precise laboratory tests. The highly qualified, specialized
and  dedicated R & D team at SIRIS IMPEX includes phytochemists, pharmacologists, microbiologists and formulating chemists.

The Company's Q.C. lab is fully equipped to monitor the herbal inputs, intermediates, and  the finished products. It is armed with HPLC, HPTLC, U.V Spectrophotometer, FTIR, Gas Chromotography, Autotitrators etc.

SIRIS IMPEX is well experienced in the cultivation of organically grown medicinal plants, manufacture and marketing export of several herbal extracts, and contract manufacture of herbal formulations.
