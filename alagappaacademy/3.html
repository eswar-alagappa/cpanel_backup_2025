About Our School

The Dr. Alagappa Academy is a Senior secondary school (Pre KG to Grade XII) that recommends NCERT Books. Our School is affiliated to CBSE, Delhi and we follow the standardized CBSE based curriculum that focuses on skills like critical thinking and problem solving through interactive events, activities and role-playing games in the classrooms.

Each student is considered uniquely nurtured with individual attention to help channelize energies and bring out their best. Housed in a serene environment, qualified teaching and non-teaching staff cater to the needs of every child.
