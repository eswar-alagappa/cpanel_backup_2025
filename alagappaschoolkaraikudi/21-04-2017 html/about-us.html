Alagappa Montessori School

Alagappa Montessori School was started on 4th September 1953. It was inaugurated by KundrakudiMahaSannithanamShri La Sri DeivaSigamaniArunachalaDesigaParamacharyaSwamigal. As it is a Montessori School the education is imparted to children in the Play Way method which works out well.

The school has a vast area for playing. There are six classrooms with sufficient ventilation and with adequate furniture. The classes function from Lower KINDER-GARTENto II Standard. A large number of play things are purchased every year to educate and entertain the kids. The kids utilize the transport facility available. They are given a strong foundation in this school and this helps them to cope with the syllabus in the various schools.

Inauguration of new buildings of Alagappa Montessori School

The new building of Alagappa Montessori School was inaugurated on 08.10.2015 by our beloved Secretary,
Dr. Mrs. Umayal Ramanathan and our beloved Chairman Thiru.R.Vairavan.

On this special occasion Trustees of Dr. Alagappa Chettiar Educational Trust, Manager of the Trust and all the Heads of Alagappa Group of Institutions, Karaikudi were present.

Alagappa Matriculation Higher Secondary School

Dr. Mrs.Umayal Ramanathan is the founder of Alagappa Matriculation Higher Secondary School.The necessity for setting up this institution arose in her mind when she thought of the Preparatory Students getting enrolled in the state stream of education. Our secretary's attention was focused in giving them an advanced stream of education namely the Matriculation stream. Now the school follows Samacheer Kalvi Syllabus prescribed by Government of Tamilnadu. Students are excelling in the academic career.

Her ponderings came into reality by the opening of Alagappa Matriculation School in 1985 with an approximate strength of 60 students. Year by year the strength went on increasing coinciding with the up gradation of standards. In the year 1989 our first batch of students appeared for the Matriculation Examination. Then the Higher Secondary stream of education was introduced in 1990. Students were admitted for three different groups with the subject combination

  • Maths , Physics, Chemistry, Biology.
  • Maths, Physics, Chemistry, Computer Science.
  • Commerce , Economics, Accountancy, Maths.

In the year 1992 the first set appeared for the Higher Secondary Examination. From 1997 onwards there has been a steady increase in the strength. Our school is located in the heart of the Karaikudi town. It has a sprawling campus of 15 acres. Our school has spacious and ventilated classrooms.Playgrounds with Shady trees around which add beauty to our school campus. The school is governed by The Dr. Alagappa Chettiar Educational Trust. The Secretary of the Trust is Dr. (Mrs.) UMAYAL RAMANATHAN

Alagappa Matriculation School has a wide and expansive campus with an excellent infrastructure. Athletics and Sports is given importance with other co-curricular activities.The school has well equipped laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, Computer Science and Biology. These are taken care of by skilled lab assistants. From this year onwards School has planned to introduce ROBOTICS with Central Governmet Tie up for our school children. Next year it will be upgraded as Space and in the third year as Aeronatics. The school is located in a serene atmosphere. It also has separate modern hostels for Girls and boys.

The school has a well equipped library ably managed by a librarian. To promote the aptitude in extensive reading among pupils weekly and monthly magazines, sports- journals and newspapers are procured for the library. A well qualified Arts and Crafts teacher is there to enhance creative thinking of the pupils and to draw out their innate talents. It is also the prime duty of a school to impart moral qualities and social responsibilities to the students. We have a regular schedule of school assembly which comprises of news reading, pledge, discourses by students themselves on various topics, Kural recital, mass drill and mass Bhajans etc.

From the year 2009 the school has a strength of about 3000 students. The school is having a dedicated team of about 100 faculty members and efficiently managed under the able leadership of a dynamic and energetic Principal Mrs. Anandhi Thyagarajan, receipent of the Tamil Nadu Government's Dr Radhakrishnan best Teacher Award.

Alagappa Montessori Hostel

HOSTEL Alagappa School Hostel was started in the year 1954. Over the years the popularity of the hostel has grown to accommodate more than 400 resident students. The children are coached by well experienced and dedicated teachers to enhance the empowerment of the children. With more than 30 caretakers including cooks, aayahs and relevant supervising staff to meet the needs of the ever expanding student resident population, separate residential quarters for Boys and Girls have been established and clearly delineated. Specific time schedule is allocated to do homework and is supervised by experienced staff members.
A recreation hall with audio visual equipment has been made available to provide entertainment for the students. All students are served with nutritious vegetarian food prepared in hygienic manner by professional cooks.
Plans are underway to build a new dormitory to house our students to compliment the academic excellence aimed by the Alagappa Preparatory, Matriculation and Higher Secondary School.
