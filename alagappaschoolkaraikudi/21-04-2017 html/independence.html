70th INDEPENDENCE DAY CELERATIONS

70th Independence Day Celebrations were held at our school campus on 15th August, 2016. The celebrations started at 7.45 AM. Our Principal, Mr. Kumaran Ganesan hoisted the National Flag. A colourful cultural event was presented by the students of our school. Our Principal, in his address highlighted the role of the various leaders who struggled hard to get the independence. He stressed the importance of discipline and good manners among the school students. He also asked the students to be a role model for the forthcoming generation.
