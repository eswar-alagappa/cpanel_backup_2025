Application for admission must be made in the prescribed form which can be obtained with the prospectus from the college office on payment of Rs.100/- in person or through post by sending a crossed bank draft drawn in favour of the Secretary along with a self addressed and stamped (Rs.10) 23x15 cm envelope. The last date for submitting the filled in application form in the college office will be the 10th day from the date of publication of Higher Secondary Examination results.

Candidates who are selected provisionally will be intimated by post to appear for an interview along with their parent and original certificates. They have to remit the fees on the day of interview without fail. The provisional admission will be cancelled if the candidate fails to turn up for the interview on the day specified. The admission is provisional and must be approved by the University.

NOTE: Fees once paid will not be refunded under any circumstances.
